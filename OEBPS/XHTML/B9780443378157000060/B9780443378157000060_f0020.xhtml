The anatomical illustration presents the anterior view of the shoulder joint with numbered labels identifying ligamentous structures, tendons, and joint components that contribute to glenohumeral articulation and stability. Label 1 marks the acromioclavicular joint. Label 2 identifies the coracoacromial ligament. Label 3 shows the cut supraspinatus tendon. Label 4 indicates the cut subscapularis tendon. Label 5 marks the tendon of the long head of the biceps brachii. Label 6 identifies the capsular ligaments that surround the glenohumeral joint. Label 7 shows the superior transverse scapular ligament. Label 8 marks the coracoclavicular ligament.
