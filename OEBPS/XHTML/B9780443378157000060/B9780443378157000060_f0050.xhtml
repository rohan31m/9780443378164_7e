The anatomical illustration presents the bones of the wrist and hand with numbered labels identifying carpal bones, metacarpals, and phalanges from anterior and posterior perspectives. Label 1 marks the scaphoid and its tubercle. Label 2 identifies the trapezium and its tubercle. Label 3 shows the trapezoid bone. Label 4 indicates the metacarpal bones. Label 5 marks the proximal phalanges bones. Label 6 identifies the middle phalanges bones. Label 7 shows the distal phalanges. Label 8 marks the capitate bone. Label 9 identifies the lunate bone. Label 10 shows the hamate bone and its hook. Label 11 marks the pisiform bone. Label 12 identifies the triquetrum bone. Label 13 shows the lunate bone again from a different perspective.
