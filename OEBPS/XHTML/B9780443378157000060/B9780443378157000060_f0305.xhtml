The anatomical illustration presents an anterior view of the hand with five labeled structures. Label 1 identifies the muscular branches of the median nerve supplying the thenar muscles and the first and second lumbrical muscles. Label 2 marks the muscular branches of the ulnar nerve that innervate the third and fourth lumbrical muscles and all interosseous muscles. Label 3 indicates the deep palmar branch of the ulnar artery and the deep branch of the ulnar nerve. Label 4 shows the cut median nerve. Label 5 denotes the ulnar nerve.
