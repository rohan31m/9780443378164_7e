The anatomical illustration presents the anterior view of scapulohumeral dissection with numbered labels identifying distinct anatomical structures in the anterior shoulder region. Label 1 identifies the subscapularis muscle. Label 2 identifies the suprascapular artery and nerve. Label 3 identifies the supraspinatus muscle tendon. Label 4 identifies the axillary nerve and posterior circumflex humeral artery passing through the quadrangular space. Label 5 identifies the triangular space. Label 6 identifies the circumflex scapular artery.
