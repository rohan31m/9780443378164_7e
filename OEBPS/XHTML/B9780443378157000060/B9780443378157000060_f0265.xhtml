The anatomical illustration presents an anterior view of the hand with eleven labeled structures. Label 1 identifies the opponens digiti minimi muscle. Label 2 marks the cut abductor digiti minimi. Label 3 indicates the ulnar nerve. Label 4 shows the ulnar artery. Label 5 denotes the pronator quadratus muscle. Label 6 highlights the radial artery. Label 7 refers to the superficial palmar branch of the radial artery. Label 8 points to the opponens pollicis muscle. Label 9 identifies the adductor pollicis. Label 10 marks the common palmar digital arteries. Label 11 shows the lumbrical muscles in a reflected position.
