The anatomical illustration presents the posterior view of the humerus and scapula with numbered labels identifying skeletal landmarks, joint surfaces, and muscular attachment sites. Label 1 marks the cut clavicle. Label 2 identifies the scapular notch. Label 3 shows the superior angle. Label 4 indicates the supraspinous fossa. Label 5 marks the spine. Label 6 identifies the infraspinous fossa. Label 7 shows the neck, which is the constricted region between the head and shaft of the humerus. Label 8 marks the medial epicondyle. Label 9 identifies the capitulum. Label 10 shows the olecranon fossa. Label 11 marks the deltoid tuberosity. Label 12 identifies the head of the humerus. Label 13 shows the acromion of the scapula.
