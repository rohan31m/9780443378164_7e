The anatomical illustration presents the bones of the elbow in extension with numbered labels identifying bony landmarks, joint surfaces, and structural contours from anterior (1 to 8) and posterior (9 to 11) perspectives. Label 1 marks the radial fossa. Label 2 identifies the capitulum. Label 3 shows the head of the radius. Label 4 indicates the radial tuberosity. Label 5 marks the coronoid fossa. Label 6 identifies the medial epicondyle. Label 7 shows the trochlea of the humerus. Label 8 marks the tuberosity of the ulna. Label 9 identifies the olecranon fossa. Label 10 shows the groove for the ulnar nerve. Label 11 marks the lateral epicondyle. Label 12 identifies the olecranon. Label 13 shows the head of radius again from the posterior view. Label 14 marks the neck of the radius.
