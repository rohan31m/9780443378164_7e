The anatomical illustration presents a transverse section of the palm with nine labeled structures. Label 1 identifies the lumbrical muscle. Label 2 marks the flexor digitorum superficialis and flexor digitorum profundus tendons to the fifth digit enclosed in a synovial sheath. Label 3 indicates the hypothenar muscles. Label 4 shows the dorsal interosseous muscles. Label 5 denotes the palmar interosseous muscles. Label 6 highlights the adductor pollicis. Label 7 refers to the flexor digitorum profundus tendon and flexor digitorum superficialis tendon to the third digit. Label 8 points to the palmar aponeurosis. Label 9 identifies the common palmar digital artery and nerve.
