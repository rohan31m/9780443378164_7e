The anatomical illustration presents the posterior shoulder muscles with numbered labels identifying superficial and deep muscle groups that span the upper back, scapula, and humerus. Label 1 marks the trapezius muscle. Label 2 identifies the deltoid muscle, which covers the shoulder joint. Label 3 shows the latissimus dorsi muscle, which arises from the lower thoracic and lumbar vertebrae. Label 4 indicates the rhomboid major muscle. Label 5 marks the infraspinatus muscle, which lies below the scapular spine. Label 6 identifies the supraspinatus muscle, which arises from the supraspinous fossa of the scapula. Label 7 shows the rhomboid minor muscle, which lies superior to the rhomboid major.
