The anatomical series of illustrations presents three cross-sectional panels of the arm, each labeled with specific structures. Label 1 identifies the biceps brachii muscle, including both short and long heads. Label 2 marks the coracobrachialis muscle. Label 3 indicates the deltoid muscle. Label 4 shows the brachialis muscle. Label 5 denotes the radial nerve. Label 6 highlights the triceps brachii muscle, including medial, lateral, and long heads. Label 7 refers to the brachioradialis muscle. Label 8 again identifies the triceps brachii muscle. Label 9 points to the humerus. Label 10 repeats the biceps brachii muscle. Label 11 corresponds to the teres major muscle. Label 12 marks the musculocutaneous nerve.
