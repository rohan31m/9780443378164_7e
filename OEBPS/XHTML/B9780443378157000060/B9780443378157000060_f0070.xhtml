The anatomical illustration presents the anterior view of metacarpophalangeal and interphalangeal ligaments with numbered labels identifying tendons, joint capsules, and palmar connective structures. Label 1 marks the joint capsule. Label 2 identifies the cut flexor digitorum superficialis tendons. Label 3 shows the flexor digitorum profundus tendons. Label 4 indicates the palmar ligaments, also called palmar plates. Label 5 marks the deep transverse metacarpal ligaments. Label 6 identifies the palmar metacarpal ligaments. Label 7 shows the palmar carpometacarpal ligaments. Label 8 marks the hook of the hamate bone.
