The anatomical illustration presents medial views of the metacarpophalangeal and interphalangeal ligaments with numbered labels identifying bones, joints, and stabilizing connective tissues during finger extension and flexion. Label 1 marks the metacarpal bone. Label 2 identifies the joint capsule. Label 3 shows the metacarpophalangeal joint. Label 4 indicates the proximal interphalangeal joint. Label 5 marks the distal interphalangeal joint. Label 6 identifies the phalangeal bones, including proximal, middle, and distal segments. Label 7 shows the palmar ligament, also called the palmar plate. Label 8 marks the collateral ligament. Label 9 identifies the accessory collateral ligament.
