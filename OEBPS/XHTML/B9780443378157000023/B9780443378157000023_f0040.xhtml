The two-panel illustration shows the sacrum and coccyx from two perspectives. The top panel shows labels 1 to 7 in an anterior inferior view. The bottom panel shows labels 8 to 15 in a posterior superior view. Label 1 indicates the lumbosacral articular surface. Label 2 points to the ala of the sacrum. Label 3 identifies the coccyx. Label 4 shows the superior articular process. Label 5 indicates the sacral promontory. Label 6 points to the anterior sacral foramina. Label 7 identifies the transverse process of the coccyx. Label 8 shows the auricular surface. Label 9 indicates the lateral sacral crest. Label 10 points to the median sacral crest. Label 11 identifies the sacral hiatus. Label 12 shows the coccygeal horn. Label 13 indicates the sacral horn. Label 14 points to the posterior sacral foramina. Label 15 identifies the facets of the superior articular processes.
