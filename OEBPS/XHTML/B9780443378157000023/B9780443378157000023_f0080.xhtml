The illustration depicts the nerves and muscles of the posterior head and upper neck with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the greater occipital nerve. Label 2 points to the great auricular nerve. Label 6 points to the obliquus inferior capitis muscle. Label 7 identifies the obliquus superior capitis muscle. Label 8 shows the suboccipital nerve. Label 9 indicates the rectus posterior major capitis muscle. Label 10 points to the rectus posterior minor capitis muscle.
