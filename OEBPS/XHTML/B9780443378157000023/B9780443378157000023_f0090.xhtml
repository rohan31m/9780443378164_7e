The anatomical illustration displays the spinal cord with its meninges and nerve structures marked with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the posterior root of the spinal nerve. Label 2 points to the spinal ganglion. Label 3 identifies the anterior ramus of the spinal nerve. Label 4 shows the posterior ramus of the spinal nerve. Label 5 indicates the spinal dura. Label 6 points to the spinal arachnoid, which is cut in this view. Label 7 identifies the subarachnoid space. Label 8 shows the pia mater overlying the spinal cord. Label 9 indicates the denticulate ligament. Label 10 points to the rootlets of the posterior root of the spinal nerve. Label 11 identifies another posterior ramus of the spinal nerve. Label 12 shows the white and gray rami communicantes.
