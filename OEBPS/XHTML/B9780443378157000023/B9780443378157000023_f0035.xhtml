The anatomical illustration shows a left lateral view and an anterior view of the vertebral column with numbered panels. The top panel, labeled left lateral view of partially sectioned in the median plane, has labels 1 to 3 and 6 to 10. The bottom panel, labeled posterior vertebral segments in anterior view, has labels 4 and 5. Label 1 indicates the anterior longitudinal ligament. Label 2 points to the intervertebral disc. Label 3 identifies the posterior longitudinal ligament. Label 4 shows the pedicle at its cut surface. Label 5 indicates the ligamentum flavum. Label 6 points to the supraspinous ligament. Label 7 identifies the interspinous ligament. Label 8 shows another view of the ligamentum flavum. Label 9 indicates the intervertebral foramen. Label 10 points to the capsule of the zygapophysial joint, which is partially opened.
