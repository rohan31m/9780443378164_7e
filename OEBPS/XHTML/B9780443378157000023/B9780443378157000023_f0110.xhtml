The anatomical illustration displays the vertebral venous plexus in two panels. The upper panel illustrates the vertebral body labels 1 to 5, and the bottom panel shows labels 6 to 9. Label 1 indicates the basivertebral vein. Label 2 points to the anterior internal vertebral venous plexus. Label 3 identifies the intervertebral vein. Label 4 shows the posterior internal vertebral venous plexus. Label 5 indicates the posterior external vertebral venous plexus. Label 6 points to the anterior segmental medullary vein. Label 7 identifies the pial venous plexus. Label 8 shows the posterior spinal vein. Label 9 indicates the anterior spinal vein.
