The anatomical illustration depicts the posterior view of the upper and lower back with numbered labels for the muscles along with their nerve supply. Label 1 indicates the rhomboid major muscle. Label 2 points to the rhomboid minor muscle. Label 3 identifies the spinal accessory nerve or cranial nerve 11, which innervates the trapezius muscle. Label 4 shows the trapezius muscle, which is reflected in this view. Label 5 indicates the posterior cutaneous branches, specifically the medial branches of the dorsal rami of the C 4 to T 6 spinal nerves. Label 6 points to the latissimus dorsi muscle. Label 7 identifies the posterior cutaneous branches, which are the lateral branches of the dorsal rami of the T 7 to T 12 spinal nerves. Label 8 shows the thoracolumbar fascia. Label 9 indicates the gluteus maximus muscle. Label 10 points to the spinous process of the T 12 vertebra.
