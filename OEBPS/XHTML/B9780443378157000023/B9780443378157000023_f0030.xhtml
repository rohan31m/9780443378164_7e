The anatomical illustration presents an intervertebral disc and the lumbar vertebrae in a left lateral view with numbered labels. The top panel shows the labels 1 and 2 on the intervertebral disc. The bottom view shows labels 3 to 13 on the lumbar vertebrae, assembled in a left lateral view. Label 1 indicates the anulus fibrosus. Label 2 points to the nucleus pulposus. Label 3 identifies the superior articular process. Label 4 shows the pedicle. Label 5 indicates the intervertebral disc. Label 6 points to the mammillary process. Label 7 identifies the inferior articular process. Label 8 shows the inferior vertebral notch. Label 9 indicates the intervertebral notch. Label 10 points to the superior vertebral notch. Label 11 identifies the transverse process. Label 12 shows the lamina. Label 13 indicates the inferior articular facet.
