This illustration shows the deep muscles located along the back of the neck and upper trunk with numbered labels identifying the muscles. Label 1 indicates the semispinalis capitis muscle. Label 2 points to the splenius capitis and splenius colli muscles. Label 3 identifies the rectus posterior minor capitis muscle. Label 4 shows the obliquus superior capitis muscle. Label 5 indicates the rectus posterior major capitis muscle. Label 6 points to the obliquus inferior capitis muscle. Label 7 identifies the spinous process of the C 7 vertebra. Label 8 shows the longissimus colli muscle. Label 9 indicates the longissimus thoracis muscle. Label 10 points to the serratus posterior inferior muscle. Label 11 identifies the spinalis thoracis portion of the erector spinae.
