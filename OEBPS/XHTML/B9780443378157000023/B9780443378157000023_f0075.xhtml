The anatomical illustration displays the deep intrinsic muscles of the back arranged along the vertebral column with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the rotatores colli muscles, including both the longi and breves fibers. Label 2 points to the rotatores thoracis muscles, also consisting of longi and breves portions. Label 3 identifies the levatores costarum muscles, which include both the breves and longi fibers. Label 4 shows the multifidus lumborum muscles. Label 5 indicates the multifidus thoracis muscles. Label 6 points to the semispinalis thoracis muscle. Label 7 identifies the semispinalis capitis muscle.
