The anatomical illustration shows the posterior view of the back with numbered labels for the muscles and structures. Label 1 indicates the splenius capitis muscle. Label 2 points to the splenius colli muscle. Label 3 identifies the rhomboid minor muscle, which is cut in this view. Label 4 shows the serratus posterior superior muscle. Label 5 indicates the rhomboid major muscle, also cut. Label 6 points to the serratus posterior inferior muscle. Label 7 identifies the erector spinae muscles. Label 8 shows the posterior layer of the thoracolumbar fascia. Label 9 indicates the spinous process of the T 12 vertebra. Label 10 points to the latissimus dorsi muscle. Label 11 identifies the spinous process of the C 7 vertebra. Label 12 shows the trapezius muscle. Label 13 indicates the semispinalis capitis muscle.
