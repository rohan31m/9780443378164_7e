The anatomical illustration of the right ankle and foot ligaments presents a lateral view with numbered labels for the related tendons and ligaments. Label 1 indicates the anterior and posterior tibiofibular ligaments. Label 2 points to the superior fibular retinaculum. Label 3 shows the inferior fibular retinaculum. Label 4 identifies the fibularis longus and brevis tendons. Label 5 indicates the bifurcate ligament, which includes the calcaneonavicular and calcaneocuboid ligaments. Label 6 points to the components of the lateral collateral ligament of the ankle, consisting of the posterior talofibular ligament, calcaneofibular ligament, and anterior talofibular ligament.
