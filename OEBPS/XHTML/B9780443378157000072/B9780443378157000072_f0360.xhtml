The anatomical illustration of the human lower limb and pelvis presents numbered labels for the nerves and veins. Label 1 indicates the femoral artery. Label 2 shows the lateral circumflex femoral artery. Label 3 points to the profunda femoris, also known as the deep femoral artery. Label 4 identifies the superior lateral genicular artery. Label 5 shows the anterior tibial artery. Label 6 indicates the fibular artery, represented as a phantom structure. Label 7 points to the posterior tibial artery, also shown in phantom. Label 8 identifies the inferior medial genicular artery, which is partially represented in phantom. Label 9 shows the popliteal artery, also indicated in phantom. Label 10 points to the medial circumflex femoral artery. Label 11 identifies the obturator artery. Label 12 shows the external iliac artery.
