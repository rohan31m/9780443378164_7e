The anatomical illustration of the thigh muscles presents the major muscles and structures in this region with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the adductor longus muscle. Label 2 points to the greater trochanter. Label 3 shows the pubic tubercle. Label 4 identifies the gracilis muscle. Label 5 indicates the vastus medialis muscle. Label 6 points to the rectus femoris tendon, which is cut in this view. Label 7 shows the iliotibial tract, also cut. Label 8 identifies the vastus lateralis muscle.
