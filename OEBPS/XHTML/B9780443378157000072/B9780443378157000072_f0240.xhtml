The posterior view of the leg has the superficial gastrocnemius muscle removed to reveal the deep structures with numbered labels showing the arteries, veins, and muscles. Label 1 indicates the popliteus muscle. Label 2 points to the plantaris tendon. Label 3 shows the posterior tibial vein and artery. Label 4 identifies the tibial nerve. Label 5 indicates the calcaneal tuberosity. Label 6 points to the gastrocnemius muscle, which is cut. Label 7 shows the head of the fibula. Label 8 identifies the biceps femoris tendon, which is cut. Label 9 indicates the plantaris muscle. Label 10 points to the common fibular nerve, which is cut. Label 11 shows the tibial nerve. Label 12 identifies the popliteal artery and vein, with the artery lying deeper and the vein more superficial.
