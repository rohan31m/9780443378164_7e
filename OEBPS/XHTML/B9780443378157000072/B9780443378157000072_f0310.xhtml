Description of

The dorsal anatomical illustration shows the bones and muscles of the toes with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the plantar interossei muscles. Label 2 points to the distal phalanx of the great toe. Label 3 shows the proximal phalanx of the great toe. Label 4 identifies the sesamoid bones. Label 5 indicates the first metatarsal bone. Label 6 points to the cuneiform bones, which include the lateral, intermediate, and medial cuneiforms. Label 7 shows the navicular bone.
