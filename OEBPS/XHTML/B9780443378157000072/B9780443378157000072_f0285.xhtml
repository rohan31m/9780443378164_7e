The plantar anatomical illustration of the human foot presents the muscles and tendons with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the quadratus plantae muscle. Label 2 points to the flexor digitorum longus tendon. Label 3 shows the flexor digitorum longus tendons. Label 4 identifies the flexor digitorum brevis tendons. Label 5 indicates the sesamoid bones. Label 6 points to the flexor hallucis longus tendon. Label 7 shows the superficial branches of the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 8 identifies the lateral plantar artery and nerve. Label 9 indicates the tibialis posterior tendon. Label 10 points again to the flexor hallucis longus tendon.
