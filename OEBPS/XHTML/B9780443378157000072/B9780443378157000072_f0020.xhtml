The anatomical illustration presents two views of the hip joint with numbered labels for the ligaments and other structures. The top panel shows an anterior view with labels 1 to 3. The bottom panel presents a posterior view with labels 4 to 8. Label 1 indicates the iliofemoral ligament, also known as the Y ligament of Bigelow. Label 2 points to the iliopectineal bursa. Label 3 shows the pubofemoral ligament. Label 4 identifies another part of the iliofemoral ligament. Label 5 indicates the ischiofemoral ligament. Label 6 points to the ischial spine. Label 7 shows the ischial tuberosity. Label 8 identifies the lesser trochanter.
