The anatomical illustration presents the lumbar spine, psoas and iliacus muscles, and the lumbar plexus. Seven structures are identified with numbered labels. The lumbar vertebrae are labeled L 1, L 2, L 3, L 4, and L 5. The thoracic spine is labeled T 12. Label 1 indicates the psoas major muscle. Label 2 points to the quadratus lumborum muscle. Label 3 shows the transversus abdominis muscle, which is cut in this view. Label 4 identifies the iliacus muscle. Label 5 indicates the iliopectineal bursa. Label 6 points to the iliofemoral ligament, also known as the Y ligament of Bigelow. Label 7 shows the greater trochanter of the femur.
