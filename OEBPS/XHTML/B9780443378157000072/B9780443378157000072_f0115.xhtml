The anatomical illustration of the thigh muscles presents the major muscles and structures in this region with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the pectineus muscle. Label 2 points to the iliopsoas, which is cut in this view. Label 3 shows the vastus intermedius muscle. Label 4 identifies the vastus lateralis muscle. Label 5 indicates the rectus femoris tendon, which is cut. Label 6 points to the sartorius tendon. Label 7 shows the vastus medialis muscle. Label 8 identifies the gracilis muscle.
