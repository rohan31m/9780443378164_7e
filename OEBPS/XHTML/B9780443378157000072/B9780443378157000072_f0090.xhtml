The anatomical illustration presents an anterior view of the muscles of the thigh with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the sartorius muscle. Label 2 points to the iliopsoas muscle. Label 3 shows the inguinal ligament. Label 4 identifies the tensor fasciae latae. Label 5 indicates the vastus medialis muscle. Label 6 points to the rectus femoris tendon, which becomes part of the quadriceps femoris tendon. Label 7 shows the patellar ligament. Label 8 identifies the vastus lateralis muscle.
