The anatomical illustration of the foot presents numbered labels for the blood vessels, nerves, and muscles of the foot. Label 1 indicates the flexor digitorum longus tendon, which has been cut to expose the underlying structures. Label 2 shows the common plantar digital nerves and arteries that supply the toes. Label 3 points to the plantar metatarsal arteries that branch out between the metatarsal bones. Label 4 identifies the plantar arch and the deep branches of the lateral plantar nerve. Label 5 shows the lateral plantar artery and nerve. Label 6 indicates the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 7 points to the deep branches of the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 8 identifies the superficial branches of the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 9 shows the proper plantar digital branch of the medial plantar artery. Label 10 indicates the proper plantar digital branches of the medial plantar nerve.
