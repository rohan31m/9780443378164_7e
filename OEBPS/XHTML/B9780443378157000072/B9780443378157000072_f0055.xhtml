The anatomical illustration presents two views of the bones of the foot with numbered labels for the associated bones. The top view is the lateral view with labels 1 to 3 and 11 to 13, and the bottom view is a medial view with labels 4 to 10. Label 1 indicates the talus, including its head and trochlea. Label 2 points to the calcaneus, showing its body and tuberosity. Label 3 shows the cuboid bone. Label 4 identifies the navicular bone. Label 5 indicates the cuneiform bones. Label 6 points to a sesamoid bone. Label 7 shows the first metatarsal bone. Label 8 identifies the calcaneus again, highlighting its tuberosity and the sustentaculum tali. Label 9 indicates the head of the talus. Label 10 points to the neck of the talus. Label 11 shows the phalangeal bones. Label 12 identifies the metatarsal bones. Label 13 also indicates the navicular bone.
