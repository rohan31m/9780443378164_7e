The anatomical illustration of the posterior view of the leg has the superficial gastrocnemius muscle removed to reveal the deep structures, with numbered labels pointing at the muscles and tendons. Label 1 indicates the plantaris muscle. Label 2 points to the common fibular nerve, which is cut. Label 3 shows the soleus muscle. Label 4 identifies the plantaris tendon. Label 5 indicates the fibularis longus tendon. Label 6 points to the fibularis brevis tendon. Label 7 shows the flexor retinaculum of the ankle. Label 8 identifies the superior fibular retinaculum. Label 9 indicates the tibial collateral ligament. Label 10 points to the fibular collateral ligament.
