The posterior view of the leg shows numbered labels for several deep and superficial structures. Label 1 indicates the flexor hallucis longus muscle, which is retracted. Label 2 points to the soleus muscle, which is cut and reflected. Label 3 shows the posterior tibial artery. Label 4 identifies the fibular artery. Label 5 indicates the tibial nerve. Label 6 points again to the posterior tibial artery. Label 7 shows the flexor digitorum longus muscle. Label 8 identifies the fibular artery. Label 9 indicates the flexor hallucis longus tendon. Label 10 points to the flexor retinaculum.
