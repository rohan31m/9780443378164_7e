The anatomical illustration presents a lateral view of the muscles of the hip and thigh with numbered labels for different related muscles. Label 1 indicates the tensor fasciae latae muscle. Label 2 points to the gluteus maximus muscle. Label 3 shows the iliotibial tract. Label 4 identifies the vastus lateralis muscle. Label 5 indicates the biceps femoris muscle, including its long and short heads. Label 6 points to the head of the fibula. Label 7 shows the patella. Label 8 identifies the rectus femoris muscle.
