The anterior anatomical illustration of the human lower leg and foot presents numbered labels for the related nerves. Label 1 indicates the common fibular nerve, which arises from spinal roots L 4 to L 5 and S 1 to S 2. Label 2 points to the superficial fibular nerve. Label 3 shows the medial dorsal cutaneous nerve. Label 4 identifies the intermediate dorsal cutaneous nerve. Label 5 indicates the lateral dorsal cutaneous nerve, which is a branch of the sural nerve. Label 6 points to the dorsal digital nerves of the foot. Label 7 shows the extensor hallucis brevis muscle. Label 8 identifies the inferior extensor retinaculum, which is partially cut. Label 9 indicates the extensor hallucis longus muscle, which is partially retracted. Label 10 points to the tibialis anterior muscle. Label 11 shows the tibia. Label 12 identifies the deep fibular nerve.
