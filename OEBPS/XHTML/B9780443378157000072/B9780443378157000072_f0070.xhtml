The anatomical diagram of the plantar ligaments and bones of the foot presents numbered for different joints, tendons, ligaments, and bones. Label 1 indicates the deep transverse metatarsal ligaments. Label 2 points to the plantar ligaments. Label 3 shows the plantar metatarsal ligaments. Label 4 identifies the fibularis longus tendon. Label 5 indicates the long plantar ligament. Label 6 points to the tibialis posterior tendon. Label 7 shows the plantar calcaneonavicular ligament, also known as the spring ligament. Label 8 identifies the tibialis anterior tendon, which is cut in this view. Label 9 indicates the sesamoid bones. Label 10 points to the interphalangeal joint. Label 11 shows the flexor hallucis longus tendon, which is also cut.
