The anatomical illustration of the thigh presents the hamstring muscles and the sciatic nerve with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the gracilis muscle. Label 2 points to the vastus intermedius muscle. Label 3 shows the vastus lateralis muscle. Label 4 identifies the rectus femoris tendon, which is cut in this view. Label 5 indicates the sartorius tendon. Label 6 points to the vastus medialis muscle.
