The plantar view of the anatomical structures of the human foot presents numbered labels for its muscles and nerves. Label 1 indicates the abductor digiti minimi muscle. Label 2 points to the lumbrical muscles. Label 3 shows the deep branch of the medial plantar nerve. Label 4 identifies the flexor digitorum brevis muscle. Label 5 indicates the abductor hallucis muscle. Label 6 points to the plantar aponeurosis, which is cut in this view.
