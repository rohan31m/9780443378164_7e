The anatomical view of the arteries of the lower limb presents numbered labels for the major blood vessels from the hip to the foot. Label 1 indicates the femoral artery. Label 2 shows the deep femoral artery. Label 3 points to the superior medial and lateral genicular arteries. Label 4 identifies the posterior tibial recurrent artery. Label 5 shows the anterior tibial artery. Label 6 indicates the fibular artery. Label 7 points to the arcuate artery. Label 8 identifies the dorsal digital arteries. Label 9 shows the dorsalis pedis artery. Label 10 indicates the popliteal artery. Label 11 points to the femoral artery passing through the adductor hiatus within the adductor magnus muscle. Label 12 identifies the medial circumflex femoral artery. Label 13 shows the external iliac artery.
