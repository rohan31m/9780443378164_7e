The anatomical posterior view of the gluteal region presents the nerves from the sacral plexus and the deep gluteal muscles with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the quadratus femoris muscle. Label 2 points to the gluteus medius muscle, which is cut. Label 3 shows the superior gemellus muscle. Label 4 identifies the gluteus minimus muscle. Label 5 indicates another part of the gluteus medius muscle, which is also cut. Label 6 points to the gluteus maximus muscle, which is cut and reflected. Label 7 shows the superior gluteal nerve. Label 8 identifies the inferior gluteal nerve. Label 9 indicates the sacrospinous ligament. Label 10 points to the sacrotuberous ligament. Label 11 shows the long head of the biceps femoris muscle. Label 12 identifies the sciatic nerve, which is cut in this view.
