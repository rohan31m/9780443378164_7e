The anatomical view of the hip and thigh regions after deep dissection presents the deep muscles and the sciatic nerve with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the gluteus minimus muscle. Label 2 points to the gluteus medius muscle, which is cut in this view. Label 3 shows the sciatic nerve, which is also cut. Label 4 identifies the quadratus femoris muscle. Label 5 indicates the gluteus maximus muscle, which is cut and reflected. Label 6 points to the greater trochanter of the femur. Label 7 shows the iliotibial tract. Label 8 identifies the semimembranosus muscle. Label 9 indicates the adductor magnus muscle. Label 10 points to the adductor minimus part of the adductor magnus muscle. Label 11 shows the ischial tuberosity. Label 12 identifies the sacrotuberous ligament.
