The anatomical illustration presents an inferior cross-sectional view of the knee joint with numbered labels for different ligaments. Label 1 indicates the popliteus tendon. Label 2 points to the fibular collateral ligament, along with its bursa. Label 3 shows the anterior cruciate ligament. Label 4 identifies the arcuate popliteal ligament. Label 5 indicates the tibial collateral ligament, which includes both superficial and deep parts. Label 6 points to the posterior cruciate ligament. Label 7 shows the suprapatellar bursa. Label 8 identifies the patellar ligament. Label 9 indicates the infrapatellar synovial fold. Label 10 points to the lateral condyle of the femur.
