The plantar view of the anatomical structures of the human foot presents numbered labels for its muscles and tendons. Label 1 indicates the abductor hallucis muscle and its tendon. Label 2 points to the superficial branches of the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 3 shows the fibrous sheaths of the flexor tendons. Label 4 identifies the flexor hallucis longus tendon. Label 5 indicates the flexor digiti minimi brevis muscle. Label 6 points to the flexor digitorum brevis muscle. Label 7 shows the abductor digiti minimi. Label 8 identifies the calcaneal tuberosity. Label 9 indicates the plantar aponeurosis, which is cut in this view.
