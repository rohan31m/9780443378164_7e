The posterior anatomical illustration of the human leg shows numbered panels for the related arteries. Label 1 indicates the popliteal artery and tibial nerve. Label 2 shows the posterior tibial artery. Label 3 points to the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 4 identifies the lateral plantar artery and nerve. Label 5 shows the fibular artery, located deep to the retracted flexor hallucis longus muscle. Label 6 indicates the fibular artery. Label 7 also points to the fibular artery. Label 8 identifies the anterior tibial artery. Label 9 shows the inferior lateral genicular artery. Label 10 indicates the superior lateral genicular artery.
