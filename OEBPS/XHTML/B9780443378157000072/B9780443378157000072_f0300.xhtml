The plantar anatomical illustration of the human foot presents the numbered labels for the muscles, tendons, and nerves. Label 1 indicates the adductor hallucis muscle, including its transverse and oblique heads. Label 2 points to the common plantar digital nerves. Label 3 shows the plantar metatarsal arteries. Label 4 identifies the fibularis longus tendon. Label 5 indicates the fibularis brevis tendon. Label 6 points to the lateral plantar artery and nerve. Label 7 shows the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 8 identifies the flexor hallucis longus tendon, which is cut.
