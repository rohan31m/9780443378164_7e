The plantar anatomical illustration of the human foot presents numbered labels for its muscles and tendons. Label 1 indicates the flexor digitorum brevis muscle. Label 2 points to the abductor hallucis muscle. Label 3 shows the flexor hallucis longus tendon. Label 4 identifies the medial head of the flexor hallucis brevis muscle. Label 5 indicates the lateral head of the flexor hallucis brevis muscle. Label 6 points to the proper plantar digital nerves. Label 7 shows the proper plantar digital arteries. Label 8 identifies the flexor digitorum brevis tendons lying over the flexor digitorum longus tendons. Label 9 indicates the flexor digiti minimi brevis. Label 10 points to the medial calcaneal branches of the tibial nerve and the posterior tibial artery.
