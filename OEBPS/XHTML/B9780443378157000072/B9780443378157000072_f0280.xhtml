The plantar anatomical illustration of the human foot presents numbered labels for its muscles, nerves, and arteries. Label 1 indicates the flexor hallucis brevis muscle, which has medial and lateral heads. Label 2 points to the lumbrical muscles. Label 3 shows the superficial branch of the lateral plantar nerve. Label 4 identifies the flexor digitorum longus tendon. Label 5 indicates the lateral plantar nerve and artery. Label 6 points to the flexor digitorum brevis, which is cut in this view. Label 7 shows the quadratus plantae muscle. Label 8 identifies the flexor hallucis longus tendon.
