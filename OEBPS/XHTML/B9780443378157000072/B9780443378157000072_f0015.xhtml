The anatomical illustration of a medial view of the hip bone or coxal bone presents structures with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the ala of the ilium, also known as the iliac fossa. Label 2 points to the arcuate line. Label 3 shows the pecten pubis, also called the pectineal line. Label 4 identifies the symphyseal surface. Label 5 indicates the obturator foramen. Label 6 points to the ramus of the ischium. Label 7 shows the ischial tuberosity. Label 8 identifies the lesser sciatic notch. Label 9 indicates the ischial spine. Label 10 points to the greater sciatic notch. Label 11 shows the articular surface of the ilium. Label 12 identifies the iliac tuberosity.
