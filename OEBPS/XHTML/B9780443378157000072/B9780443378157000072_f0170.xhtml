The anatomical deep dissection of the gluteal region presents the nerves from the sacral plexus and the deep gluteal muscles with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the obturator internus muscle, which is cut to reveal the nerve to the quadratus femoris. Label 2 points to the piriformis muscle. Label 3 shows the superior gluteal nerve. Label 4 identifies the inferior gluteal nerve as it passes to the gluteus maximus muscle. Label 5 indicates the pudendal nerve, which supplies the perineum. Label 6 points to the sacrotuberous ligament. Label 7 shows the ischial tuberosity. Label 8 identifies the sciatic nerve, which is cut in this view. Label 9 indicates the quadratus femoris muscle. Label 10 points to the greater trochanter of the femur.
