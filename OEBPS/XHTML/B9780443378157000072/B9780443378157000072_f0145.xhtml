The posterior anatomical view of the gluteal and thigh regions presents the superficial muscles with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the gluteus maximus muscle. Label 2 points to the adductor magnus. Label 3 shows the semitendinosus muscle. Label 4 identifies the long head of the biceps femoris muscle. Label 5 indicates the semimembranosus muscle. Label 6 points to the popliteal vessels and tibial nerve located in the popliteal fossa. Label 7 shows the gastrocnemius muscle, including its medial and lateral heads. Label 8 identifies the sartorius muscle. Label 9 indicates the gracilis muscle.
