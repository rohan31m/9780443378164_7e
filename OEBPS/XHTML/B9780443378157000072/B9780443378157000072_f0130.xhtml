The anatomical illustration of the gluteal and thigh regions after deep dissection presents the muscles and the proximal part of the posterior thigh with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the obturator externus muscle. Label 2 points to the adductor longus, which is cut and reflected. Label 3 shows the pectineus muscle, also cut and reflected. Label 4 identifies the quadratus femoris muscle. Label 5 indicates another view of the pectineus muscle, which is cut and reflected. Label 6 points to the adductor brevis, which is cut and reflected. Label 7 shows the adductor longus again, cut and reflected. Label 8 identifies the tendon of the adductor magnus muscle, inserting on the adductor tubercle of the medial epicondyle of the femur.
