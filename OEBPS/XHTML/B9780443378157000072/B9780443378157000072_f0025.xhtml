The anatomical illustration shows the hip joint structure with bone and soft tissue components in an open joint lateral view with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the lunate surface of the acetabulum. Label 2 points to the articular cartilage. Label 3 shows the head of the femur. Label 4 identifies the ligament of the head of the femur, which is cut in this view. Label 5 indicates the lesser trochanter of the femur. Label 6 points to the ischial tuberosity. Label 7 shows the transverse acetabular ligament. Label 8 identifies the obturator membrane. Label 9 indicates the obturator artery. Label 10 points to the acetabular labrum. Label 11 shows the anterior inferior iliac spine. Label 12 identifies the anterior superior iliac spine.
