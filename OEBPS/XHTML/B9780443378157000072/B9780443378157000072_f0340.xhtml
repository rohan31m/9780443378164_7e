The posterior view of the human lower limb presents a dermatome map with numbered labels marking the dermatomes. Label 1 indicates the twelfth thoracic vertebra, T 12. Label 2 points to the lumbar vertebrae from L 1 to L 5. Label 3 shows the first sacral vertebra, S 1. Label 4 identifies the second sacral vertebra, S 2. Label 5 indicates the fifth lumbar vertebra, L 5. Label 6 points to the third sacral vertebra, S 3. Label 7 shows the fourth sacral vertebra, S 4. Label 8 identifies the fifth sacral vertebra, S 5. Label 9 indicates the coccygeal nerve, Co 1, which is the single pair of coccygeal spinal nerves. Label 10 points to the fifth lumbar nerve, L 5, which supplies the left calf. Label 11 shows the first sacral nerve, S 1, which supplies the left calf, heel, and little toe. Label 12 identifies the nerves L 4, L 5, and S 1, which supply the sole of the foot.
