The posterior view of the leg has the superficial gastrocnemius muscle removed to reveal the deep structures with numbered labels identifying the muscles, tendons, arteries, and veins. Label 1 indicates the soleus muscle. Label 2 points to the plantaris muscle. Label 3 shows the popliteal artery, vein, and tibial nerve. Label 4 identifies the tibial collateral ligament. Label 5 indicates the popliteus muscle. Label 6 points to the plantaris tendon. Label 7 shows the soleus muscle inserting into the calcaneal tendon. Label 8 identifies the posterior tibial vein and artery, along with the tibial nerve.
