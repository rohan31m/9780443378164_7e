The anatomical illustration presents an anterior view with numbered labels for the muscles of the thigh. Label 1 indicates the vastus lateralis muscle. Label 2 points to the gracilis muscle. Label 3 shows the adductor longus. Label 4 identifies the pubic tubercle. Label 5 indicates the iliopsoas. Label 6 points to the tensor fasciae latae. Label 7 shows the sartorius muscle. Label 8 identifies the rectus femoris tendon, which becomes part of the quadriceps femoris tendon. Label 9 indicates the iliotibial tract. Label 10 points to the patella. Label 11 shows the patellar ligament. Label 12 identifies the sartorius tendon.
