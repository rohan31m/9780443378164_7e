The cross-sectional superior view of the knee joint is depicted with numbered labels for ligaments and tendons. Label 1 indicates the lateral meniscus. Label 2 points to the iliotibial tract. Label 3 shows the patellar ligament. Label 4 identifies the anterior cruciate ligament. Label 5 indicates the synovial membrane, which lines the joint capsule. Label 6 points to the medial meniscus. Label 7 shows the tibial collateral ligament, whose deep part is bound to the medial meniscus. Label 8 identifies the oblique popliteal ligament. Label 9 indicates the semimembranosus tendon. Label 10 points to the posterior cruciate ligament. Label 11 shows the popliteus tendon. Label 12 identifies the fibular collateral ligament, along with its associated bursa.
