The plantar view of the anatomical structures of the human foot presents the related muscles and tendons with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the flexor digiti minimi brevis muscle. Label 2 points to the sesamoid bones. Label 3 shows the lateral and medial heads of the flexor hallucis brevis muscle. Label 4 identifies the abductor hallucis muscle, which is cut. Label 5 indicates the medial plantar artery and nerve. Label 6 points to the flexor hallucis longus tendon. Label 7 shows the posterior tibial artery and tibial nerve as they divide. Label 8 identifies the lateral plantar nerve and artery.
