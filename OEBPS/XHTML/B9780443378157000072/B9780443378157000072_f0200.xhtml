The anatomical illustration of the lateral view of the leg and foot details the muscles and tendons of the lateral and anterior compartments with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the fibularis longus muscle along with its tendon. Label 2 points to the lateral head of the gastrocnemius muscle. Label 3 shows the head of the fibula. Label 4 identifies the common fibular nerve. Label 5 indicates the iliotibial tract. Label 6 points to the patellar ligament. Label 7 shows the tibialis anterior muscle. Label 8 identifies the extensor digitorum longus muscle.
