The posterior view of the gluteal and thigh regions presents numbered labels for the superficial muscles, vessels, and nerves. Label 1 indicates the gluteus medius muscle, which is covered by the gluteal aponeurosis. Label 2 points to the long head of the biceps femoris muscle. Label 3 shows the iliotibial tract. Label 4 identifies the semitendinosus muscle. Label 5 indicates the short head of the biceps femoris muscle. Label 6 points to the popliteal vessels and tibial nerve located in the popliteal fossa. Label 7 shows the sartorius muscle. Label 8 identifies the gracilis muscle. Label 9 indicates the gluteus maximus muscle.
