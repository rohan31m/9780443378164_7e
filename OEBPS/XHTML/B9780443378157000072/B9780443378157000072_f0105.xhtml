The anatomical illustration presents an anterior view of the muscles of the thigh with numbered labels that expose the deeper muscles. Label 1 indicates the vastus intermedius muscle. Label 2 points to the vastus lateralis muscle. Label 3 shows the vastus medialis muscle. Label 4 identifies the rectus femoris tendon, which is cut in this view. Label 5 indicates the patella. Label 6 points to the sartorius tendon. Label 7 shows the patellar ligament. Label 8 identifies the vastoadductor membrane. Label 9 indicates the gracilis muscle. Label 10 points to the pectineus muscle.
