The anatomical illustration of the thigh with a deep dissection view presents the major muscles, vessels, and nerves of the femoral triangle and adductor canal with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the adductor brevis muscle. Label 2 points to the adductor magnus. Label 3 shows the femoral artery and vein, which are cut in this view. Label 4 identifies the adductor magnus tendon. Label 5 indicates the medial patellar retinaculum. Label 6 points to the patellar ligament. Label 7 shows the rectus femoris muscle, which is cut. Label 8 identifies the femoral nerve, arising from spinal levels L 2 to L 4. Label 9 indicates the obturator nerve, showing both its anterior and posterior branches, also derived from L 2 to L 4.
