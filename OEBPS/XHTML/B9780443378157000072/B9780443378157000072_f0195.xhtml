The anatomical illustration depicts three sequential transverse cross-sections of the thigh, from superior to inferior, and details the musculature and neurovasculature with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the sartorius muscle. Label 2 points to the tensor fasciae latae. Label 3 shows the vastus lateralis muscle. Label 4 identifies both the short and long heads of the biceps femoris muscle. Label 5 indicates the vastus intermedius muscle. Label 6 points again to the biceps femoris muscle. Label 7 shows the semitendinosus muscle. Label 8 identifies the semimembranosus muscle. Label 9 indicates the sartorius muscle seen from another position. Label 10 points to the adductor magnus. Label 11 shows the gracilis muscle. Label 12 identifies the great saphenous vein. Label 13 indicates the femoral artery and vein. Label 14 points again to the semitendinosus muscle. Label 15 shows the gracilis muscle once more. Label 16 identifies the great saphenous vein as it runs along the medial thigh.
