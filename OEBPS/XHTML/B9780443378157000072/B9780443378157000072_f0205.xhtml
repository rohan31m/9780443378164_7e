The anatomical illustration of the lateral view of the leg and foot details the muscles and tendons of the lateral and anterior compartments with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the fibularis brevis muscle along with its tendon. Label 2 points to the soleus muscle located beneath the gastrocnemius. Label 3 shows the lateral head of the gastrocnemius muscle. Label 4 identifies the long and short heads of the biceps femoris muscle. Label 5 indicates the iliotibial tract. Label 6 points to the tibialis anterior muscle. Label 7 shows the superficial fibular nerve, which is cut in this view. Label 8 identifies the superior extensor retinaculum. Label 9 indicates the inferior extensor retinaculum. Label 10 points to the tendon of the fibularis brevis muscle.
