The anatomical view of the hip and thigh regions presents numbered labels for the deep muscles and the sciatic nerve. Label 1 indicates the superior gemellus muscle. Label 2 points to the inferior gemellus muscle. Label 3 shows the greater trochanter of the femur. Label 4 identifies the gluteus maximus muscle, which is cut in this view. Label 5 indicates the iliotibial tract. Label 6 points to the short head of the biceps femoris muscle. Label 7 shows the long head of the biceps femoris muscle, which is cut. Label 8 identifies the semitendinosus muscle, also cut. Label 9 indicates the gracilis muscle. Label 10 points to the adductor magnus muscle.
