Description of

The anatomical illustration of the muscles of mastication and associated structures with numbered labels for the temporomandibular joint and several muscles and ligaments of the jaw and cheek. Label 1 indicates the lateral pterygoid muscle, and label 2 points to the articular disc of the temporomandibular joint. Label 3 identifies the sphenomandibular ligament, while label 4 shows the medial pterygoid muscle. Label 5 indicates the pterygomandibular raphe, and label 6 points to the buccinator. Label 7 identifies the parotid duct of Stensen.
