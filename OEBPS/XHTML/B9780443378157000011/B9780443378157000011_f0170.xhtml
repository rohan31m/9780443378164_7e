The anatomical view of the pharynx and larynx structures shows the posterior aspect of the pharynx with muscles and cartilage from the skull base to the esophagus, with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the inferior pharyngeal constrictor, and label 2 points to the cricopharyngeal part of the inferior pharyngeal constrictor. Label 3 identifies the esophagus. Label 4 shows the posterior cricoarytenoid muscle. Label 5 indicates the lamina of the thyroid cartilage. Label 6 points to the internal branch of the superior laryngeal nerve. Label 7 identifies the epiglottis. Label 8 shows the lingual tonsil. Label 9 indicates the choana.
