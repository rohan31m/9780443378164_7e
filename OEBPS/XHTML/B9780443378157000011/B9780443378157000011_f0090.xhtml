The anatomical illustration shows a lateral view of a skull with muscles with numbered labels for the muscles, a nerve, and bones that work together to help in chewing, speaking, and making facial expressions. Label 1 indicates the buccinator, and label 2 points to the zygomaticus minor muscle. Label 3 identifies the zygomaticus major muscle, while label 4 shows the depressor anguli oris. Label 5 indicates the depressor labii inferioris, and label 6 points to the mentalis muscle. Label 7 identifies the levator nasolabialis, and label 8 shows the levator labii superioris.
