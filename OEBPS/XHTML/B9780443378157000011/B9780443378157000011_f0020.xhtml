The midsagittal view of the human skull with numbered labels shows cranial bones and facial bones. Label 1 indicates the sphenoid bone with its greater wing, lesser wing, Sella turcica, and sinus of the sphenoid bone. Label 2 points at the frontal bone containing the sinus of the frontal bone. Label 3 identifies the ethmoid bone with its perpendicular plate, while label 4 shows the maxilla with the incisive canal and palatine process. Label 5 indicates the vomer, label 6 points to the palatine bone, and label 7 identifies the hypoglossal canal. Label 8 shows the groove for the transverse sinus, and label 9 indicates the occipital bone. Label 10 points to the lambdoid suture, while label 11 identifies the temporal bone with its squamous and petrous parts. Label 12 shows the grooves for branches of the middle meningeal vessels. Label 13 indicates the coronal suture, and label 14 points to the parietal bone.
