Description of

The anatomical illustration shows the arrangement and types of adult teeth in both the upper and lower jaws with numbered labels. The top panel, labeled upper permanent teeth, has labels 1 to 4. Label 1 indicates the incisive fossa, and label 2 points to the palatine process of the maxillary bone. Label 3 identifies the horizontal plate of the palatine bone, while label 4 shows the greater and lesser palatine foramina. The bottom panel, labeled lower permanent teeth, has labels 5 to 12. Label 5 indicates the central incisor, one on each side of the midline, and label 6 points to the lateral incisor. Label 7 identifies the canine tooth. Label 8 shows the first premolar tooth. Label 9 indicates the second premolar tooth. Label 10 points to the first molar tooth. Label 11 identifies the second molar tooth. Label 12 shows the third molar tooth.
