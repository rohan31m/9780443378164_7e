The anatomical illustration presents a lateral view of the human head and neck with numbered labels for the superficial sensory nerves and associated musculature. Label 1 indicates the supraorbital nerve. Label 2 points to the infraorbital nerve. Label 3 identifies the mental nerve, while label 4 shows the buccal nerve. Label 5 indicates the auriculotemporal nerve. Label 6 points to the supraclavicular nerves arising from C 3 and C 4. Label 7 identifies the transverse cervical nerve, including C 2 and C 3, while label 8 shows the great auricular nerve, including C 2 and C 3. Label 9 indicates the lesser occipital nerve or C 2, and label 10 points to the greater occipital nerve or C 2.
