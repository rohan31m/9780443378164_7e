The anatomical illustration of the arteries of the root of the neck and scapular region displays a view of the subclavian artery and its branches with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the vertebral artery. Label 2 points to the costocervical trunk. Label 3 identifies the supreme intercostal artery. Label 4 shows the internal thoracic artery. Label 5 indicates the suprascapular artery. Label 6 points to the thyrocervical trunk. Label 7 identifies the right common carotid artery. Label 8 shows the transverse cervical artery. Finally, label 9 indicates the inferior thyroid artery.
