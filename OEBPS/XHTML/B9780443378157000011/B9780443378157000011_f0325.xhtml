The anatomical illustration shows the venous drainage and some arteries on the side of the human head and neck, depicting the superficial and deep veins with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the pterygoid venous plexus. Label 2 points to the submandibular gland. Label 3 identifies the superior laryngeal vein. Label 4 shows the middle thyroid vein. Label 5 indicates the inferior thyroid veins. Label 6 points to the subclavian vein. Label 7 identifies the thyroid gland. Label 8 shows the internal jugular vein. Label 9 indicates the common facial veins. Label 10 points to the cut view of the external jugular vein. Label 11 identifies the retromandibular vein. Label 12 shows the superficial temporal vein and artery.
