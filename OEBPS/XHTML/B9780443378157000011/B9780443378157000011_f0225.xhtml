The anatomical illustrations of the larynx show two panels with numbered labels for different muscles, ligaments, and cartilages. The top panel shows a lateral dissection view with labels 1 to 5 and 9. The bottom panel shows a superior view with labels 6 to 8 and 10. Label 1 indicates the aryepiglottic part of the oblique arytenoid muscle. Label 2 points to the posterior cricoarytenoid muscle. Label 3 identifies the thyroepiglottic part of the thyroarytenoid muscle. Label 4 shows the external part of the thyroarytenoid muscle. Label 5 indicates the lateral cricoarytenoid muscle. Label 6 points to the vocalis muscle. Label 7 identifies the vocal ligament. Label 8 shows the conus elasticus. Label 9 indicates the thyroid articular surface. Label 10 points to the lamina of the cricoid cartilage.
