The anatomical illustration of the pharynx and surrounding structures, with numbered labels, shows a posterior view of the pharyngeal and palatal muscles, with the skull base above and pharyngeal constrictors below. Label 1 indicates the tensor veli palatini, and label 2 points to the basilar part of the occipital bone. Label 3 identifies the cartilaginous part of the auditory tube, while label 4 shows the superior pharyngeal constrictor. Label 5 indicates the palatopharyngeus muscle. Label 6 points to the musculus uvulae. Label 7 identifies the pterygoid hamulus. Label 8 shows the cut view of medial pterygoid muscle.
