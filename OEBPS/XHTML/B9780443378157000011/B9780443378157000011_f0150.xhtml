The anatomical illustration shows a posterior view with numbered labels for muscles, cartilages, glands, and bones, which together form the framework of the throat and upper airway. Label 1 indicates the levator veli palatini, and label 2 points to the choanae. Label 3 identifies the cut view of superior pharyngeal constrictor, while label 4 shows the palatopharyngeus muscle. Label 5 indicates the musculus uvulae, and label 6 points to the pterygoid hamulus. Label 7 identifies the cut view of medial pterygoid muscle, while label 8 shows the tensor veli palatini muscle and tendon. Label 9 indicates the cut view of levator veli palatini. Label 10 points to the basilar part of the occipital bone. Label 11 identifies the cartilaginous part of the auditory tube.
