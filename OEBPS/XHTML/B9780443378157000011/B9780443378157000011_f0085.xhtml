The anatomical illustration shows muscles, which are responsible for facial movements and neck movements, with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the occipitalis muscle, and label 2 points to the auricularis posterior muscle. Label 3 identifies the auricularis superior muscle, while label 4 shows the auricularis anterior muscle. Label 5 indicates the levator nasolabialis, which is partially cut away, and label 6 points to the levator labii superioris. Label 7 identifies the zygomaticus minor muscle. Label 8 shows the zygomaticus major muscle. Label 9 indicates the risorius muscle. Label 10 points to the depressor anguli oris. Label 11 identifies the platysma.
