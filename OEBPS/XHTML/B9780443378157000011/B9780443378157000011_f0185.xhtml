The anatomical illustration shows a deep anterior view of the neck muscles with numbered labels for muscles, along with bones, nerves, and blood vessels. Label 1 indicates the sternocleidomastoid muscle, and label 2 points to the posterior belly of the digastric muscle. Label 3 identifies the mylohyoid muscle, while label 4 shows the anterior belly of the digastric muscle. Label 5 indicates the stylohyoid muscle, and Label 6 points to the cut view of platysma muscle.
