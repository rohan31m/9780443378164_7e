The anatomical illustration displays two panels showing the nerves of the nasal cavity and septum, with numbered panels for different nerves. The top panel labeled lateral wall of nasal cavity shows numbered labels 1 to 3, and 6 to 11. The bottom panel labeled nasal septum shows labels 4 and 5. Label 1 indicates the olfactory bulb. Label 2 points to the lateral nasal branch of the anterior ethmoidal nerve. Label 3 identifies the palatine nerves, which include the greater and lesser palatine nerves. Label 4 shows the nasopalatine nerve. Label 5 indicates the palatine branch of the nasopalatine nerve in the incisive canal. Label 6 points to the nerve or vidian of the pterygoid canal. Label 7 identifies the deep petrosal nerve. Label 8 shows the greater petrosal nerve. Label 9 indicates the pterygopalatine ganglion. Label 10 points to the maxillary nerve or cranial nerve V 2 with the sphenopalatine foramen dissected away. Label 11 identifies the olfactory tract.
