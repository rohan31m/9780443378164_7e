The illustration shows a lateral view of the parotid gland and associated neurovascular and muscular structures on the right side of the human face and upper neck with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the parotid gland. Label 2 points to the facial nerve emerging from the stylomastoid foramen. Label 3 identifies the sternocleidomastoid muscle. Label 4 shows the external jugular vein. Label 5 indicates the temporal branches of the facial nerve. Label 6 points to the parotid duct of Stensen. Label 7 identifies the masseter muscle. Label 8 shows the facial artery and vein. Finally, label 9 indicates the cervical branch of the facial nerve.
