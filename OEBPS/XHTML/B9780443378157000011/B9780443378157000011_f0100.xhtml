The anatomical illustration shows eye muscles with numbered labels for the eye and the muscles around it that control eye movement. The top panel, labeled right lateral view, has labels 1 to 3 and 6 to 8. Label 1 indicates the superior rectus muscle, and label 2 points to the medial rectus muscle. Label 3 identifies the inferior rectus muscle. Label 6 points to the inferior oblique muscle. Label 7 identifies the cut view of lateral rectus muscle, and label 8 shows the superior oblique muscle. The bottom panel shows a superior view with labels 3 to 5. Label 4 shows the superior oblique muscle. Label 5 indicates the lateral rectus muscle.
