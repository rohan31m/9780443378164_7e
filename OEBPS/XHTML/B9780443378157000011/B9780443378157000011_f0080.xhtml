The anatomical illustration shows muscles of the face and neck that help with expressions, chewing, speaking, and head movement, with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the cut view of frontalis muscle, and label 2 points to the orbicularis oculi muscle. Label 3 identifies the orbicularis oris muscle, while label 4 shows the mentalis muscle. Label 5 indicates the depressor labii inferioris, and label 6 points to the depressor anguli oris.
