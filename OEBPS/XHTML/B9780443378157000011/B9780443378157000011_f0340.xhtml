The coronal dissection of the superior part of the head details the meninges and associated structures, with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the diploic veins. Label 2 points to the superior sagittal sinus. Label 3 identifies the granular foveola, which is an indentation of the skull caused by an arachnoid granulation. Label 4 shows the lateral lacuna. Label 5 indicates the inferior sagittal sinus. Label 6 points to the middle meningeal artery and vein. Label 7 identifies the cranial pia. Label 8 shows the subarachnoid space. Label 9 indicates the cranial arachnoid. Label 10 points to the periosteal layer of the cranial dura. Label 11 identifies the bridging vein. Label 12 shows the arachnoid granulation.
