The anatomical illustration shows numbered labels for muscles, as well as related anatomical features involved in chewing and facial movement. Label 1 indicates the temporalis muscle, and label 2 points to the zygomatic arch. Label 3 identifies the styloid process, while label 4 shows the cut-away view insertion of the masseter muscle. Label 5 indicates the parotid duct of Stensen, and label 6 points to the buccinator. Label 7 identifies the mental foramen. Label 8 shows the orbicularis oris muscle.
