The anatomical illustration of the nerves and arteries of the pterygopalatine fossa shows a frontal view of the skull with structures within and related to the right pterygopalatine fossa exposed with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the nasopalatine nerve. Label 2 points to the pterygoid canal, located behind the ganglionic branches connecting the maxillary nerve or cranial nerve V 2) and the pterygopalatine ganglion. Label 3 identifies the maxillary nerve or cranial nerve V 2. Label 4 shows the pterygopalatine ganglion. Label 5 indicates the infraorbital nerve. Label 6 points to the superior alveolar nerve. Label 7 identifies the greater and lesser palatine nerves. Label 8 shows the lesser and greater palatine arteries. Label 9 indicates the anterior and middle superior alveolar arteries. Label 10 points to the superficial temporal artery. Label 11 identifies the maxillary artery. Label 12 shows the descending palatine artery. Label 13 indicates the infraorbital artery. Label 14 points to the sphenopalatine artery.
