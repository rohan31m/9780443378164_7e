The sagittal section shows the dural venous sinuses and internal veins of the human head, detailing the venous drainage system, with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the inferior sagittal sinus. Label 2 points to the anterior and posterior intercavernous sinuses. Label 3 identifies the inferior petrosal sinus. Label 4 shows the sigmoid sinus. Label 5 indicates the transverse sinus, and Label 6 points to the occipital sinus. Label 7 identifies the confluence of sinuses. Label 8 shows the superior sagittal sinus. Label 9 indicates the straight sinus. Label 10 points to the great cerebral vein of Galen. Label 11 identifies the superior petrosal sinus. Label 12 shows the falx cerebri.
