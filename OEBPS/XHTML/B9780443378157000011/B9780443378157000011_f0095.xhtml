The anatomical illustration shows numbered labels for muscles, along with the eyeball structures and nerves. Label 1 indicates the levator palpebrae superioris, and label 2 points to the optic nerve or cranial nerve 2 within its meningeal sheath. Label 3 identifies the inferior rectus muscle, while label 4 shows the inferior oblique muscle. Label 5 indicates the lateral rectus muscle, and label 6 points to the superior rectus muscle. Label 7 identifies the superior oblique muscle, and label 8 shows the trochlea or pulley.
