The anatomical illustration shows the lacrimal apparatus and associated structures and ducts of the eye and nasal cavity with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the orbital part of the lacrimal gland. Label 2 points to the plica semilunaris and lacrimal lake. Label 3 identifies the lacrimal caruncle. Label 4 shows the inferior lacrimal papilla and punctum. Label 5 indicates the opening of the nasolacrimal duct. Label 6 points to the inferior nasal meatus. Label 7 identifies the cut view of the inferior nasal concha. Label 8 shows the nasolacrimal duct. Label 9 indicates the middle nasal concha. Label 10 points to the lacrimal sac. Label 11 identifies the lacrimal canaliculi. Label 12 shows the superior lacrimal papilla and punctum. Finally, label 13 indicates the palpebral part of the lacrimal gland.
