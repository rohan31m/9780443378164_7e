The anatomical illustration shows a lateral view with numbered labels for the main muscles and structures that control chewing and facial expressions. Label 1 indicates the masseter, and label 2 points to a cut view of the parotid duct. Label 3 identifies the buccinator, while label 4 shows the depressor anguli oris. Label 5 indicates the depressor labii inferioris, and label 6 points to the levator nasolabialis. Label 7 identifies the levator labii superioris, while label 8 shows the zygomaticus minor muscle. Label 9 indicates the zygomaticus major muscle, and label 10 points to the temporalis muscle. Label 11 identifies the temporal fascia, and label 12 shows the zygomatic arch.
