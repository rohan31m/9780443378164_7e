The anatomical illustration of the lateral aspect of the face displays numbered labels for the parotid gland and various facial nerve branches. Label 1 indicates the facial nerve emerging from the stylomastoid foramen. Label 2 points to the parotid gland. Label 3 identifies the parotid duct of Stensen. Label 4 shows the cervical branch of the facial nerve. Label 5 indicates the marginal mandibular branch. Label 6 points to the facial artery and vein. Label 7 identifies the buccal branches. Label 8 shows the zygomatic branches. Label 9 indicates the temporal branches.
