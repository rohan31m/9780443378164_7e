The anatomical illustration presents numbered labels for the nerves of the orbit and associated ganglia in a sagittal view, in addition to the various associated fibers. Label 1 indicates the ciliary ganglion, and Label 2 points to the short ciliary nerves. Label 3 identifies the infraorbital nerve, while Label 4 shows the inferior branch of the oculomotor nerve. Label 5 indicates the pterygopalatine ganglion, and Label 6 points to the maxillary nerve (cranial nerve V2). Label 7 identifies the mandibular nerve (cranial nerve V3), while Label 8 shows the abducens nerve (cranial nerve VI). Label 9 indicates the trochlear nerve (cranial nerve IV), and Label 10 points to the oculomotor nerve (cranial nerve III). Label 11 identifies the ophthalmic nerve (cranial nerve V1), while Label 12 shows the frontal nerve (cut). The efferent and afferent fibers are marked in different colored bold lines. The sympathetic fibers are marked in dashed lines, and the parasymphathetic fibers in dotted lines.
