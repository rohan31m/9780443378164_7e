The anatomical illustration with an inferior view of the human skull shows numbered labels for bones that form the floor of the skull. Label 1 indicates the maxilla with its incisive fossa, palatine process, and zygomatic process. Label 2 points to the zygomatic bone, while label 3 identifies the sphenoid bone with its medial plate, lateral plate, and greater wing. Label 4 shows the temporal bone, including its zygomatic process, mandibular fossa, styloid process, bony external acoustic meatus, and mastoid process. Label 5 indicates the stylomastoid foramen, and label 6 points to the parietal bone. Label 7 identifies the occipital bone, including the occipital condyle, basilar part, foramen magnum, and external occipital protuberance. Label 8 shows the foramen lacerum, while label 9 indicates the vomer. Label 10 points to the choanae of the cranium. Label 11 identifies the palatine bone with its horizontal plate.
