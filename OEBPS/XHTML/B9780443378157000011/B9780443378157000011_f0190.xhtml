The anatomical illustration displays the anterior neck with numbered labels for the major blood vessels, muscles, and glands located in the cervical region. Label 5 indicates the cut view of sternohyoid muscle. Label 6 points to the thyroid gland. Label 7 identifies the cricoid cartilage. Label 8 shows the thyrohyoid muscle. Label 9 indicates the internal jugular vein. Label 10 points to the mylohyoid muscle. Label 11 identifies the anterior belly of the digastric muscle. Label 12 shows the hyoid bone. Label 13 indicates the facial vein. Label 14 points to the thyrohyoid muscle.
