The anatomical illustration displays a lateral view of the neck muscles with numbered labels for the sternocleidomastoid, suprahyoid, and infrahyoid groups. Label 1 indicates the omohyoid muscle, and label 2 points to the sternohyoid muscle. Label 3 identifies the thyrohyoid muscle, while label 4 shows the stylohyoid muscle. Label 5 indicates the posterior belly of the digastric muscle. Label 6 points to the sternocleidomastoid muscle. Label 7 identifies the scalenus muscles, including the posterior, medius, and anterior parts.
