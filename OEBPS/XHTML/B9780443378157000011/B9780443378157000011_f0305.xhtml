The anatomical illustration of the carotid artery and its branches displays numbered labels for the major arteries and their branching pattern in the neck region. Label 1 indicates the superficial temporal artery. Label 2 points to the occipital artery. Label 3 identifies the internal carotid artery. Label 4 shows the external carotid artery. Label 5 indicates the common carotid artery. Label 6 points to the superior thyroid artery along with the superior laryngeal artery. Label 7 identifies the ascending pharyngeal artery. Label 8 shows the lingual artery. Label 9 indicates the facial artery. Label 10 points to the posterior auricular artery. Finally, label 11 identifies the maxillary artery.
