The anatomical illustration shows a lateral view of the human skull with numbered labels identifying major cranial and facial bones. Label 1 indicates the parietal bone, label 2 points to the coronal suture, label 3 identifies the sphenoid bone, and label 4 shows the lacrimal bone. Label 5 indicates the maxilla with its frontal process and alveolar process, while label 6 points to the zygomatic bone. Label 7 identifies the styloid process of the temporal bone, and label 8 shows the occipital bone with its external occipital protuberance. Label 9 indicates the lambdoid suture, label 10 points to the temporal bone, including its squamous part, zygomatic process, bony external acoustic meatus, and mastoid process, and label 11 identifies the pterion.
