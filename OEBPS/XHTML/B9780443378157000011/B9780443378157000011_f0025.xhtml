The anatomical illustration with a sagittal view presents numbered labels for the human nasal cavity and surrounding cranial and facial bones. Label 1 indicates the frontal bone with its sinus. Label 2 points to the nasal bone. Label 3 identifies the major alar cartilage. Label 4 shows the maxilla with its frontal process, incisive canal, palatine process, and alveolar process. Label 5 indicates the inferior nasal concha bone. Label 6 points to the palatine bone with its perpendicular and horizontal plates. Label 7 identifies the sphenoid bone with its sinus, medial pterygoid plate, lateral pterygoid plate, and pterygoid hamulus. Label 8 shows the basilar part of the occipital bone. Label 9 indicates the opening into the maxillary sinus. Label 10 points to the sphenopalatine foramen. Label 11 identifies the Sella turcica for the pituitary gland. Label 12 shows the ethmoid bone with its bony middle nasal concha, cribriform plate, and bony superior nasal concha. Label 13 indicates the lacrimal bone.
