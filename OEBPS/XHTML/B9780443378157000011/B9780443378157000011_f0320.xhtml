The anatomical illustration of the arteries of the head and neck displays a lateral view of the right side of the head and neck, with the arteries and some deep structures exposed with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the temporalis fascia, which covers the temporalis muscle. Label 2 points to the buccopharyngeal fascia, covering the superior pharyngeal constrictor muscle. Label 3 identifies the cut view of the parotid gland. Label 4 shows the superficial investing cervical fascia. Label 5 indicates the alar fascia. Label 6 points to the esophagus. Label 7 identifies the trachea. Label 8 shows the carotid sheath. Label 9 indicates the fascia of the infrahyoid muscles. Label 10 points to the cut view of the pretracheal fascia. Label 11 identifies the mylohyoid muscle. Label 12 shows the cut view of the submandibular gland.
