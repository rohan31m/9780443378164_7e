The anatomical illustration shows the posterior view of the mandible with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the condylar process, and label 2 points to the neck of the mandible. Label 3 identifies the pterygoid fovea, while label 4 shows the inferior alveolar foramen or mandibular foramen. Label 5 points to the mylohyoid groove, and label 6 indicates the submandibular fossa. Label 7 shows the mylohyoid line, and label 8 identifies the sublingual fossa. Label 9 points to the superior and inferior mental spines. Label 10 indicates the lingula. Label 11 shows the mandibular notch. Label 12 identifies the coronoid process.
