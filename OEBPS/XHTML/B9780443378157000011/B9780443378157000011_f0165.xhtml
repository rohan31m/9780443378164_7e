The anatomical illustration of the pharyngeal muscles and skull base shows numbered labels for muscles, cartilages, bones, and glands that form the back of the throat and voice box. Label 1 indicates the superior pharyngeal constrictor, and label 2 points to the stylopharyngeus muscle. Label 3 identifies the medial pterygoid muscle, while label 4 shows the stylohyoid muscle. Label 5 indicates the posterior belly of the digastric muscle. Label 6 points to the pharyngobasilar fascia. Label 7 identifies the cartilaginous part of the auditory tube, while label 8 shows the levator veli palatini. Label 9 indicates the palatopharyngeus muscle. Label 10 points to the epiglottis.
