The anatomical illustration shows a sagittal view of the tongue with numbered labels for muscles, bones, and tendons that work together to move the tongue and jaw for chewing, swallowing, and speaking. Label 1 indicates the genioglossus muscle, and label 2 points to the geniohyoid muscle. Label 3 identifies the middle pharyngeal constrictor, while label 4 shows the stylopharyngeus muscle. Label 5 indicates the stylohyoid muscle, and label 6 points to the styloglossus muscle. Label 7 identifies the palatopharyngeus muscle, while label 8 shows the palatoglossus muscle.
