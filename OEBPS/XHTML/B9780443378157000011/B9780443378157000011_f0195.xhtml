The anatomical illustration presents a deep anterior view of the neck muscles, larynx, trachea, and veins with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the sternothyroid muscle, and label 2 points to the thyrohyoid muscle. Label 3 identifies the posterior belly of the digastric muscle, while label 4 shows the stylohyoid muscle. Label 5 indicates the mylohyoid muscle. Label 6 points to the anterior belly of the digastric muscle. Label 7 identifies the thyrohyoid membrane. Label 8 shows the hyoid bone. Label 9 indicates the thyroid cartilage. Label 10 points to the superior belly of the omohyoid muscle. Label 11 identifies the cricoid cartilage. Label 12 shows the thyroid gland or isthmus. Label 13 indicates the trachea. Label 14 points to the cut view of sternohyoid.
