The anatomical lateral view of the submandibular and parotid regions shows a human head cut in a sagittal plane, displaying a cross-section of the face, mouth, and neck on the right side with numbered labels for salivary glands, vessels, and nerve structures. Label 1 indicates the branches of the facial nerve. Label 2 points to the transverse facial artery. Label 3 identifies the parotid duct. Label 4 shows the sublingual caruncle with the opening of the submandibular duct. Label 5 indicates the sublingual gland. Label 6 points to the submandibular duct. Label 7 identifies the cut view of the mylohyoid muscle. Label 8 shows the submandibular gland, including its deep and superficial lobes. Label 9 indicates the common facial vein. Label 10 points to the parotid gland. Finally, label 11 identifies the superficial temporal artery and vein.
