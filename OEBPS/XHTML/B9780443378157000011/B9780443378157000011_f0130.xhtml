The anatomical illustration shows a posterior superior view of the oral cavity floor structures with numbered labels for different muscles, nerves, and arteries. Label 1 indicates the geniohyoid muscles, and label 2 points to the genioglossus muscles. Label 3 identifies the sublingual caruncles with the openings of the submandibular ducts, while label 4 shows the sublingual gland. Label 5 indicates the lingual nerve, and label 6 points to the inferior alveolar nerve. Label 7 identifies the chorda tympani, while label 8 shows the maxillary and inferior alveolar arteries. Label 9 indicates the external carotid artery, and label 10 points to the lingual artery.
