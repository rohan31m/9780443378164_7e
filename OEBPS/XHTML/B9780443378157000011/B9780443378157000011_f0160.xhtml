The anatomical illustration of the oral cavity and palate structures shows an anterior view into the open mouth, with numbered labels for the structures in the palate and surrounding muscles. Label 1 indicates the musculus uvulae, and label 2 points to the palatopharyngeus muscle. Label 3 identifies the palatoglossus muscle, while label 4 shows the superior pharyngeal constrictor. Label 5 indicates the pterygomandibular raphe, and label 6 points to the buccinator. Label 7 identifies the greater palatine foramen. Label 8 shows the greater palatine artery and nerve. Label 9 indicates the palatine process of the maxillary bone. Label 10 points to the incisive papilla. Label 11 identifies the palatine glands with skin removed. Label 12 shows the pterygomandibular raphe.
