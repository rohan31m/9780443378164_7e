The anatomical illustration shows the left lateral section of the human head, depicting the cranial nerves, ganglia, and their interconnections. Label 1 indicates the nerve of the pterygoid canal or Vidian nerve. Label 2 points to the deep petrosal nerve. Label 3 identifies the greater petrosal nerve. Label 4 shows the otic ganglion. Label 5 indicates the chorda tympani nerve. Label 6 points to the inferior alveolar nerve. Label 7 identifies the superior cervical ganglion. Label 8 shows the lingual nerve. Label 9 indicates the submandibular ganglion. Label 10 points to the pterygopalatine ganglion. Finally, label 11 identifies the ciliary ganglion.
