The anatomical illustration shows the posterior views of the cervical region of the spine, with numbered labels for the neck vertebrae, ligaments, and arteries. The top panel includes labels 1 to 9. Label 5 indicates the ligamenta flava, and label 6 points to the spinous process. Label 7 identifies the occipital bone, and label 8 shows another view of the transverse process of the atlas or C 1. Label 9 indicates the vertebral artery. The bottom panel has labels 10 to 21. Label 10 points to the capsule of the atlantooccipital membrane. Label 11 identifies the posterior atlantooccipital membrane. Label 12 shows the ligamenta flava. Label 13 indicates the nuchal ligament. Label 14 points to the spinous process of the C 7 vertebra. Label 15 identifies the spinous process of the T 1 vertebra. Label 16 shows the T 1 vertebra, while label 17 indicates the zygapophysial joints between C 4 and C5 and C5 and C6. Label 18 points to the vertebral artery. Label 19 identifies the anterior longitudinal ligament. Label 20 shows the body of the axis. Label 21 indicates the atlas or C 1.
