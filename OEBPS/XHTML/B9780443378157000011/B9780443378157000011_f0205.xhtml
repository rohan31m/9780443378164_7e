The anatomical illustration displays the muscles of the floor of the mouth and the suprahyoid and infrahyoid muscles in a lateral view with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the stylohyoid muscle, and label 2 points to the thyrohyoid muscle. Label 3 identifies the omohyoid muscle. Label 4 shows the sternohyoid muscle. Label 5 indicates the body of the hyoid bone. Label 6 points to the mylohyoid raphe. Label 7 identifies the trochlea for the intermediate tendon of the digastric muscle. Label 8 shows the anterior belly of the digastric muscle. Label 9 indicates the mylohyoid muscle. Label 10 points to the hyoglossus muscle. Label 11 identifies the ramus of the mandible. Label 12 shows the coronoid process. Label 13 indicates the mastoid process. Label 14 points to the posterior belly of the digastric muscle.
