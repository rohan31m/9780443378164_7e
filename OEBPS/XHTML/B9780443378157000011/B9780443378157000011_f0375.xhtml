The anatomical sagittal cross-section of the human nasal and pharyngeal cavities shows the structures of the head and upper respiratory tract with numbered labels. Label 1 indicates the frontal sinus. Label 2 points to the middle nasal concha. Label 3 identifies the middle nasal meatus. Label 4 shows the inferior nasal concha. Label 5 indicates the palatine process of the maxillary bone. Label 6 points to the horizontal plate of the palatine bone. Label 7 identifies the opening of the auditory tube. Label 8 shows the soft palate. Label 9 indicates the torus tubarius. Label 10 points to the pharyngeal tonsil, which is called the adenoid when enlarged. Label 11 identifies the pituitary gland. Label 12 shows the sphenoidal sinus. Finally, label 13 indicates the superior nasal concha.
