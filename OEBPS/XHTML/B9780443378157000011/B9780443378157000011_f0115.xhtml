The anatomical illustration shows an anterior view of the lower jaw with numbered labels for the pterygoid muscles and associated structures. Label 1 indicates the medial pterygoid muscle, and label 2 points to the sphenomandibular ligament. Label 3 identifies a cut view of the levator veli palatini, while label 4 shows the cut view of tensor veli palatini. Label 5 indicates the lateral pterygoid muscle, and label 6 points to the pterygoid hamulus.
