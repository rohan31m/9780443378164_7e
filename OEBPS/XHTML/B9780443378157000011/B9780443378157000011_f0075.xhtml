The anatomical illustration, with numbered labels, shows cartilages and membranes that make up the framework of the larynx. The anterior and posterior views both show labels 1 to 3, 9, and 10, and 4, 6, 8 are identified on the anterior view on the left, and 5 and 7 are on the posterior view on the right. Label 1 indicates the epiglottic cartilage, and label 2 points to the hyoid bone. Label 3 identifies the thyrohyoid membrane, while label 4 shows the superior thyroid notch. Label 5 indicates the arytenoid cartilage, and label 6 points to the lamina of the thyroid cartilage. Label 7 identifies the vocal ligament. Label 8 shows the median cricothyroid ligament. Label 9 indicates the cricoid cartilage. Finally, label 10 points to the trachea.
