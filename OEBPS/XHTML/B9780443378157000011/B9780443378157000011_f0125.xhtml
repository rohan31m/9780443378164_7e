The anatomical illustration shows numbered labels for muscles, along with bones and tendons that support the tongue and aid in swallowing and speaking. Label 1 indicates the mylohyoid muscle, and label 2 points to the hyoglossus muscle. Label 3 identifies the lesser horn of the hyoid bone, while label 4 shows the body of the hyoid bone. Label 5 indicates the trochlea for the intermediate tendon of the digastric muscle, and label 6 points to the greater horn of the hyoid bone. Label 7 identifies the posterior belly of the digastric muscle, while label 8 shows the stylohyoid muscle. Label 9 indicates the mylohyoid raphe, and label 10 points to the anterior belly of the digastric muscle.
